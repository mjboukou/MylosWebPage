About US

Mulos kouiasas is an old water mill hidden in the nature!

Near to the village kallarites up on the road you can find a sign "Mylos kouiasas" thats us! All started in 2018 when we had the idea to present this place to the world.After days of work we made a small coffee shop in the nature without disturbing anything from the old water mill. Now you can visit Mylos kouiasas and take a brake to drink some coffee or to eat something while your mind is relaxed by the sounds of nature.
